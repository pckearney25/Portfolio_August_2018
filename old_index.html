About Me

My interests lie at the intersection of business, science, and technology, and I thrive on working with people to solve the problems that hinder the development of new products—anything from research tools to new pharmaceuticals, or web-based applications. In my career, I have been a pharmaceutical research director, have founded a chemical scientific research company, and after recently completing the Full-Stack Flex-Coding Boot Camp program offered by University of Kansas, am currently seeking new career opportunities in full-stack web-development. Personally, I have a strong interest in making scientific information more useful, accessible, and understandable to everyone, and in my spare time you can find me working over on Bee-Z Street, a gardening web application I’m continuing to develop so everyday people can make more exciting landscapes at home by using native plants. Forever the scientist, I'm also experimenting to develop a new line of home gardening products.
